DeepRacing is a group of
SF Bay area deep learning wizards & hardware hackers, trying to push the autonomous robot/vehicle revolution, closer to reality - one hack at-a-time...


Optimizing for performance and cost, we are riding the wave of innovation that is coming from the 1/10th-1/16th scale autonomous vehicles (i.e. hacked remote control cars).  This can be amazing fun, and useful for those wanting to dive into machine vision, machine learning, deep learning, autonomous vehicles, and in general *robotics*
Inspired by:
